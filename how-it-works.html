How It Works

SALIPO

Organization Governance:

SALIP Organization is governed by the Board of Directors. The Board of Directors oversee organization values and sets strategic direction. Also, they are responsible to monitor organization performance and its management. They are also, concerned with appointing an Executive Director. Day to day operations of the organization is vested to the Saving Lives Planet Organization management team.

OBJECTIVES

General Objective

To bring together synergistic relationship of multi-sectoral stakeholders for enhancing ability of the targeted communities to tackle their own economic, social and environmental problems for sustainable community development.

Specific Objectives are:

1. To promote Gender equality.
2. To educate the deprived and under privilege individuals about their basic human rights and find basis to incorporate their efforts and mobilization to achieve maximum output.
3. To facilitate access to credit and motivate communities group saving and leading for income generating activities and economic empowerment.
4. To conduct mean research, analyse data and establish baseline information for establishing new project and sharing with other stake holders.
5. To devise and organize for youth and children particularly to their rights to acquire education and improve their health and earnings.
6. To conserve natural resources, environmental protection, water supply and hygienic sanitation.
7. To fight against drug abuse and launching programs aimed at rehabilitation of drug addicts.
8. To establish working relations amongst national and local government authority and like-minded non-government organizations.
9. To assist in disaster prediction and mitigation through various relief and rehabilitation programs.
10. Improve health conditions by providing basic health and hygiene education and services to the communities residing in neglected areas.

About Us

Saving Lives Planet Organization is an independent non-government and non-profit organization with its headquarter in Dodoma City, Tanzania. Established in November 2018, under the NGO Act 2002 and registered in July 25th 2019 with registration number OONGO/R/0236.

Our motto is capacity building and poverty alleviation through services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society.
