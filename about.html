About The Organization

Approach

During the process of social mobilization, we adopt a participatory, self-help, self-sustainable approach through community involvement and formation of volunteer groups in remote rural areas.

Target groups

SALIP basically works for the poorest of the poor, vulnerable and marginalized individuals in the rural areas of the country. Its target groups include the girls, women, youth and children in the rural communities of Tanzania who are still living in miserable conditions and deprived of very basic necessities of life. Besides, SALIP Organization also focuses upon the urban population living in slums. For achieving its goal and objective, Saving Lives Planet Organization target groups include health workers and other staff related to health issues, teachers, pupils, environmentalists and stakeholders in the field of health, education and environment.

Philosophy

Sustainable rural development is a dynamic process posing various needs at different phases of time. These can be dealt with continuous interventions based on designed frameworks that incorporate the requirements projected by the communities and with the help of the communities by strategizing for public-private partnership.

Our Vision

Creates enabling environment that promote realization of socio-cultural respect, economic and protection of fundamental human rights among youth and women in the society.

Goal

To contribute in improving the living standard of the vulnerable and marginalized communities within Tanzania through community mobilization, capacity building, advocacy and strengthening of institutions at the grassroots level.

Strategy

A sustainable strategy is devised to achieve the maximum, a need based and right based approach ascertains problem with fully community involvement and mobilizing indigenous and local resources.

About Us

Saving Lives Planet Organization is an independent non-government and non-profit organization with its headquarter in Dodoma City, Tanzania. Established in November 2018, under the NGO Act 2002 and registered in July 25th 2019 with registration number OONGO/R/0236.

Our motto is capacity building and poverty alleviation through services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society.
