About Us

Saving Lives Planet Organization is an independent non-government and non-profit organization with its headquarter in Dodoma City, Tanzania. Established in November 2018, under the NGO Act 2002 and registered in July 25th 2019 with registration number OONGO/R/0236.

Our motto is capacity building and poverty alleviation through services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society.
