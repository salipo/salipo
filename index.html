Our Mission To contribute to communities change through participatory project approaches, promotion of gender equality through a value system based, to help build a better world where people are self-fulfilled as individuals.


What we do?

Influenced by the new development paradigm, which puts people before things, adopting a participatory approach in every sphere of our work. We guarantee the traditional knowledge of the rural poor is valued and respected, and there is confidence that rural people are capable of self-reliant organization. On a practical level, the fundamental thrust of this approach is decentralization and empowerment. For a community to be able to work for its own development and reduce its dependency there is a need for full involvement at all stages of the program cycle. The participation of communities in identification, planning, management, implementation and monitoring progress of program is a key factor in our work. Internally a participatory approach is adopted in managing the affairs the organization. Externally, our organization role as a “change agent” helps to develop community management structures that are capable of managing their own development. This increases the ability of the community to demand accountability in all their affairs, from government and community leaders. It reinforces the community's belief in the value of their own development choices and efforts. These community management structures are not parallel structures, but are built upon traditional institutions, promoting equitable representation and participation Human resource development is an essential part of our development work. This means ensuring that the community is trained as well as organized, to maximize the potential of the community to take hold of its own development. SALIP Organization strongly believes in maximizing its impact by transferring field experiences into knowledge to influence the development policies and practices of NGOs, donors and government agencies. This is important for working towards the long-term goal of eradicating poverty.

SALIP Organization follows the following strategy in implementation of its programs and projects.

Community mobilization:

While implementing programs/projects as a first step we undertake community mobilization so that to raise awareness in community on health and hygiene, importance of education in modern day affairs, gender related issues, socio-cultural and economic development and environmental protection. For this purpose SALIP Organization management holds meetings with the community members, conducts training/capacity building sessions and also launched campaigns.

Advocacy:

Advocacy is achieving better results in the field of social/community development. SALIP Organization believes in advocacy and therefore, the management always strongly advocate for the issues related to our organization. To achieve this objective, SALIP through its strong advocacy as approaches the line departments of Government of Tanzania, Non Government Organisations (NGOs), Community Based Organizations (CBOs), Civil Society Organizations (CSOs), and members of the community. SALIP Organization promotes the right based approach and supports dialogue as an important tool for achieving the above mentioned objectives.

About Us

Saving Lives Planet Organization is an independent non-government and non-profit organization with its headquarter in Dodoma City, Tanzania. Established in November 2018, under the NGO Act 2002 and registered in July 25th 2019 with registration number OONGO/R/0236.

Our motto is capacity building and poverty alleviation through services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society.
